Introduction

Welcome to Aircraft Checklist Pro(V1.2.0)

Aircraft Checklist Pro gives you access to aircraft- specific checklists for all included Microsoft Flight Simulator aircraft. The 80+ page document provides you with everything you need to know to get your aircraft from a cold & dark state to the skies and then back to the ground again before shutting down.

All default aircraft are included in Aircraft Checklist Pro, including the newly added 40th Anniversary Edition planes and helicopters.

Our custom-made application will allow you to access the checklist directly from within the simulator itself. Find the aircraft you’re flying and follow the tried and tested checklists just as real- world pilots would do. You will also have access to the PDF document, which can be saved to any device of your choice.

Accessing Aircraft Checklist Pro In-Sim

You can access Aircraft Checklist Pro any time from within the simulator.

1 - When in-flight, hover your mouse above to the top of your screen to show the in-sim tool bar.

2 - Select the icon for ‘Aircraft Checklist Pro’

3 - A new in-sim pop-up will appear with Aircraft Checklist Pro

You can use this tool to search, find keywords and skip to relevant pages.

Accessing Aircraft Checklist Pro as a PDF

You can access Aircraft Checklist Pro at any time as a PDF by navigating to the product folder.

[Community Folder]\sofly-aircraft-checklist-pro\ inc\pdf\

You can then share or copy this PDF to any device of your choosing.

Note, the PDF can be found for PC users only.
